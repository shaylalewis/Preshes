About Me

Hi, I'm Kitsune 23 Star also known as Preshes! I'm a freelance artist that loves designing characters! I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil and always trying to improve. Outside of designing characters I also love to make comics.

When I'm not creating original art I love to make fanart, especially of fighting game characters. I also attend tournaments and conventions where I sell my art. One of my goals is to eventually make characters for a fighting game that people will love for years to come.

Please check out my shop if you're interested in buying any of my art or in commissioning me
